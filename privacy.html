We collect information that you voluntarily provide when using our Services, including:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, company name, job title, phone number, and password when you create an account

1.2 Information We Collect Automatically

When you access our Services, we automatically collect certain information, including:

  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with our Services, including features used, pages visited, and actions taken
  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, device type, unique device identifiers, and IP address

  • Cookies and Tracking Technologies: Information collected through cookies, web beacons, and similar technologies

1.3 Cloud Infrastructure Data

To provide our cloud cost management services, we collect data about your cloud infrastructure, including:

  • Cloud resource usage and configuration data
  • Cost and billing information from your cloud providers
  • Resource tags and metadata
  • Performance and utilization metrics

This data is collected through secure API integrations with your cloud providers (such as AWS, Google Cloud, and Microsoft Azure) using credentials you provide.

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Provide Services: To operate, maintain, and deliver the features and functionality of our Services
